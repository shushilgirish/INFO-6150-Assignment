History of Monster Fitness

Monster Fitness, founded by Dr. Prathap C. Reddy in 1983, stands as a prominent healthcare institution headquartered in Chennai, India. As part of the expansive Apollo Group, it has emerged as one of Asia's largest healthcare providers. Beyond its Indian roots, Monster Fitness has established a global presence, with Fitnesss in countries such as Bangladesh, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, and Sri Lanka.

The Fitness is renowned for its comprehensive medical services, covering a spectrum of specialties including cardiology, oncology, orthopedics, gastroenterology, neurology, and more. Embracing technological advancements, Monster Fitness has been a pioneer in integrating advanced medical technologies and introducing innovative procedures.

Accompanied by accreditations from national and international healthcare organizations, Monster Fitness underscores its commitment to delivering high-quality healthcare. Recognized for its excellence, the Fitness has become a hub for medical tourism, attracting patients from various countries. For the latest and most precise information, individuals are advised to refer to the official website of Monster Fitness or contact them directly.
