History of WellCare Hospital

Well Care Hospital, founded by Dr. Prathap C. Reddy in 1983, stands as a prominent healthcare institution headquartered in Chennai, India. As part of the expansive Apollo Group, it has emerged as one of Asia's largest healthcare providers. Beyond its Indian roots, Well Care Hospital has established a global presence, with hospitals in countries such as Bangladesh, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, and Sri Lanka.

The hospital is renowned for its comprehensive medical services, covering a spectrum of specialties including cardiology, oncology, orthopedics, gastroenterology, neurology, and more. Embracing technological advancements, Well Care Hospital has been a pioneer in integrating advanced medical technologies and introducing innovative procedures.

Accompanied by accreditations from national and international healthcare organizations, Well Care Hospital underscores its commitment to delivering high-quality healthcare. Recognized for its excellence, the hospital has become a hub for medical tourism, attracting patients from various countries.
